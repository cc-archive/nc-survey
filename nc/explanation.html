This Survey Explained

This survey is designed to collect information from the public, especially CC license adopters and CC license users of NonCommercially licensed material, about what they believe qualifies as a noncommercial use.

The survey is based on the Discussion Draft of the NonCommercial Guidelines. However, it is still a work in progress and does not definitively state what is and what is not a noncommercial use nor what a particular licensor believed noncommercial meant when they applied a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial, Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike or Attribution-NonCommercial-NoDerivatives license to their work.

Consequently, if you are using a work that has been licensed under one of these licenses, you should not rely on the guidelines or this survey to confirm whether or not your proposed use is or is not a noncommercial use. If you are uncertain as to whether your use is noncommercial or not, your best course of action at this stage, is to contact the licensor and ask them.

Once Creative Commons has sufficient survey data, we will then be reviewing the data and working with the community to identify the best way of incorporating this data into its licensing system, if that is possible and advisable.
